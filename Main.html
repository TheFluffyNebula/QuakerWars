            A long time ahead in a galaxy not far away...

            An asymmetrical time vortex in Founders has caused one side to progress forward
            and the other remember the old ways. 

            The technologically advanced Frankatrons have developed a new technology called 
            the astrano core that allows them to produce unlimited energy. 
            
            Meanwhile, Kingborough's wizards have been developing spells to counteract 
            the astrano core in the Hege Academy for Sorcery.

            Kingborough's fairy authorities are pursuing a cyborg spy as he returns from The Wizards' Commons 
            to inform the Frankatrons of the newest prophecy, one that could alter the war forever...
